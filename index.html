About Us


4-Seasons is located in the midst of Himalayas, along the Chandigarh-Shimla National Highway in the Mushroom City of India - Solan. With just 2 kms away from the city, this hotel is a perfect blend of natural and city life. A place where you're near to the city, but away from the city noises, in the calm and soothing environment where you can enjoy the perfect city view.


We believe in client satisfaction and hospitality and leave no stone unturned to give our guests the experience they deserve
